Mom's Roasted Sausage and Vegetables in the Air Fryer

A simple meal for those crazy nights!

  1. Ingredients

    • 1 pound smoked sausage - cut into 1" rounds
    • 2 small zuchinni - cut into 1" half moons
    • 1 small yellow squash - cut into 1" half moons
    • 2 peppers, 1 green & 1 red - cut into 1" pieces
    • 1 red pepper - cut into 1" pieces
    • 1 small purple onion - cut into 1" pieces
    • 2 tablespoons olive oil
    • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
    • 1 teaspoon onion powder
    • 1 tablepoon Italian seasoning

  2. Instructions

    1. Prehead the air fryer to 400℉
    2. Add the sausage and vegetables to a mixing bowl.
    3. Pour the olive oil over the veggies and sausage and toss to coat everything in the oil.
    4. Pour in the garlic powder, onion powder and Italian seasoning and toss well, making sure everything is coated.
    5. Spray the air fryer basket with olive oil spray, and then working in batches add half of the sausage and vegetable mixture to the air fryer basket in an even layer.
    6. Close the air fryer and cook for 17 minutes, shaking the basket halfway through the cooking time. Continue until you have cooked all of the sausage and vegetables.
    7. Remove and serve hot.

You can use any quick-cooking veggies you like in this recipe. Possible variations are chopped broccoli, cauliflower, carrots, mushrooms, or green beans.

You can use any type of sausage and change up the seasoning as well. Possible variation is New Orleans sausage with cajun seasoning.
