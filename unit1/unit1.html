Manicotti with Sausage and Three Cheeses

Ingredients

  • 12 manicotti shells
  • 1/2 pound Italian Sausage, cooked
  • 4 cups shredded mozzarella cheese, divided
  • 8 ounces soft cream cheese with onions and chives*
  • 3 cups marinara sauce
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese

*Make your own flavored cream cheese. Soften 8 ounces cream cheese and fold in 1 tablespon each finely chopped onions and chives. Blend well. Refrigerate in an airtight container for up to 2 weeks.

Procedure

  1. Cook Italian Sausage in pan. Drain and crumble. Set aside.
  2. While Italian Sausage is cooking, cook manicotti in a large pot of boiling water according to package directions. Drain and rinse with cold water; drain again.
  3. Preheat oven to 350℉. Spray a baking dish with cooking spray.
  4. Mix Italian Sausage and 3 cups mozzarella with soft cream cheese. Stuff the manicotti; place in prepared baking dish.
  5. Spoon marinara sauce over manicotti.
  6. Sprinkle manicotti with remaining mozzarella. Bake for 15 minutes. Sprinkle with Parmesan. Bake for 10 minutes longer.

Quick Tips

Make Ahead: Freeze stuffed manicotti without the sauce in an airtight container for up to 1 month. Defrost; add sauce, then bake as recipe directs in step 4.

Variations: (1) Thaw 1/2 cup frozen shopped spinach and add it to the cheese mixture in step 2 for extra nutrients. (2) Don't like Italian Sausage? Simply leave it out of the recipe. (3) Mince 1/2 cup fresh mushrooms and add it to the cheese mixture in step 2 for extra flavor.

Helpful Hint: Manicotti must be drained very well before it is stuffed. Otherwise, the filling will become soggy and the sauce diluted. To quickly and easily remove excess moisture, place drained manicotti on paper towels.

Stuffing Hint: Don't have time to stuff manicotti shells by hand? Simply split them lengthwise, spoon in the cheese mixture, then fold over to enclose the filling.
